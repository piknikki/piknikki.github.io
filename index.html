I am Nikki Petersen. I live in the Denver metro area and I have spent a little less than a year teaching myself Software Development. My experience includes: HTML/CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, JQuery, Node.js, Express, MySQL, Sequelize, Mongo/Mongoose, Handlebars, EJS, React, and a handful of other random packages/libraries/etc. I spend my professional free time continually learning. Right now, I'm adding testing methods and practices to my skillset. Personal free time is spent outdoors with my kids, learning and creating and generally engaging in shenanigans.

On a more personal note: the last year or so has been a huge growth spurt for me, in terms of self-awareness. After being married to a man for 10-ish years, divorcing him, and then his death, I was finally able to come out and be myself for the first time since childhood. It seems like a lot of women have recently been coming out after being married and having children, and I have to say that my experience has been comparatively smooth. I'm lucky to have great friends who support me no matter what (unconditional love ftw), and I've never had to deal with the chaos of coming out to my horrible ex and his horrible family. So many women coming out late in life have very different outcomes. For me, it's been humbling to be so loved and cared for by my friends and family. Love is love. I often like to represent my journey with a "backward" rainbow of hearts because I've come out in a sort of reverse order, having lived one life for so long and finally being able to be my full self.
